Who are we?

We are McGill Robotics, the largest, most diverse, and most prolific student engineering design team at McGill University. We live for robotics. We are renowned competitors in many robotics competitions around the world.

What is RoboHacks?

RoboHacks is a robotics-focussed hackathon, created by McGill Robotics, to bring together more than 150 students in building robots over the course of a weekend. Whether you are an expert or an outright newcomer, we look forward to sharing our knowledge, guidance and tools to help you build some really cool things. Join our mailing list here.

FAQ

What is a "hackathon"?

A "hackathon" is an invention marathon. Hackathons bring together large groups of students who are interested in technology, allowing them to make their ideas into reality. In this case, we’re going to provide you with the tools and knowledge you need to make robots!

Who can come?

Cegeps and Undergraduates are both welcome to apply. We also welcome some graduate students.

What if we have no experience?

All the better! In fact, we are organizing this event primarily to help spread our knowledge and introduce beginners to robotics.

What do we have to pay?

Nothing :) Just get here and we’ll give you free food, drinks, robotics, tools, and all the goodies you could ever want.

Are there groups?

We will help form groups at the event. You can choose to apply as a group (indicated in the application), or if you'd prefer, can also work alone!
