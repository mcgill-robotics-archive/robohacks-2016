Welcome to the Spectacular World of Robotics

Want to build your very own Robot? Team up with McGill Robotics this March, at RoboHacks: a 24-hour robotics marathon.

F.A.Q.

Who can attend Robohacks?

Are you in Cegep? We want you. Are you in Undergrad? We want you. Grad student? We want you too!

Do I need previous experience?

NO! In fact, the less you know the more you will learn. McGill Robotics comprises many very knowledgeable people, who will act as mentors, and host tutorials and workshops throughout the event.

Is hardware provided?

Of course. We have many generous sponsors who are also excited about this event, and are helping us provide the gadgets and tools you need to make your robot. We also allow BYOH (bring your own hardware).
